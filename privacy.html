hiTTChaRide Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

What We Do

hiTTChaRide shows real-time locations of Toronto Transit Commission (TTC) buses that are out of service (deadheading). This helps transit operators catch buses returning to service.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No personal data collected
  • No location tracking of users
  • No analytics or usage data stored

Public Transit Data

We display publicly available TTC vehicle location data from Toronto Transit Commission's official API. This data is already public information.

How the App Works

  • Our server monitors public TTC bus locations 24/7
  • Identifies buses that have gone out of service
  • Your app displays this processed information
  • No personal data involved in this process

Third-Party Services

The app uses Google Maps for displaying locations (subject to Google's privacy policy).

Children's Privacy

Since we collect no personal information, the app is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

Any updates will be posted here with a new "last updated" date.
