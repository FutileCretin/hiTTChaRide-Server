hiTTChaRide Privacy Policy Last updated: May 21, 2026 APPLICATION OF POLICY This privacy policy applies to the hiTTChaRide mobile application and its cloud service. hiTTChaRide shows real-time locations of Toronto Transit Commission (TTC) buses that are out of service (deadheading) to help transit operators identify buses returning to service. INFORMATION WE COLLECT AND HOW WE USE IT We do not collect any personal information whatsoever. Specifically, we do not collect: • User accounts, registration, or login information • Personal data of any kind • Your device location or GPS coordinates • Usage analytics or tracking data • Device identifiers or advertising IDs PUBLIC TRANSIT DATA We display publicly available TTC vehicle location data obtained from the Toronto Transit Commission's official public API. This transit data is already publicly accessible information and contains no personal details. HOW THE SERVICE WORKS Our cloud server continuously monitors public TTC bus locations, identifies vehicles that have gone out of service, validates them through a filtering process, and provides this processed information to the mobile app. No personal data is involved at any stage of this process. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES The app uses Google Maps to display bus locations. Google Maps is subject to Google's own privacy policy, which you can review on Google's website. DATA RETENTION Since we collect no personal information, there is no personal data to retain or delete. Transit location data is processed in real-time and not permanently stored. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY Since we collect no personal information of any kind, the app is safe for users of all ages and complies with children's privacy regulations. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY Any updates to this privacy policy will be posted here with a new "last updated" date. We recommend checking this policy periodically for any changes. CONTACT INFORMATION If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Google Play Store listing for hiTTChaRide.
